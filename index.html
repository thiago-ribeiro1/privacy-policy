Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the Music Player application respects and protects your privacy in accordance with applicable privacy standards.

  • No personal data is collected from users. The app does not request, store or transmit any information that can be used to identify the user individually.
  • The Music Player app does not send emails or newsletters to users.
  • The app does not use digital analytics software, such as Google Analytics, for traffic tracking purposes.
  • The app does not display ads, does not use Facebook Pixel, and does not perform any retargeting advertising activities.
  • The app only uses Local Storage for functionality, without employing cookies, web beacons, sessions, or Google Maps APIs.
  • The app does not collect geolocation data, access contacts, use the camera, or access the user's photo gallery.
  • The application is available for all age groups, including users under 13 years old. However, as no personal data is collected, it complies with children's online privacy regulations without specific restrictions.
  • The app does not comply with CalOPPA, CCPA or GDPR, since it does not collect or process personal data that would trigger obligations under these laws.

This policy is effective from July 15, 2025. If there are any questions about this document or the practices of the application, contact thiagoribeiroramos_@outlook.com.
